Our Painting Process

At Gold Lion Painting of Florida LLC, we follow a streamlined, detail-oriented process designed to deliver exceptional results with minimal disruption. Here’s what you can expect at every stage of your project: 

1. Setup

We begin by preparing your space to ensure full protection and efficiency.

Interior:

Furniture and décor are carefully moved or covered. Floors and surfaces are fully protected using clean drop cloths and plastic coverings.

Exterior:

All surfaces are thoroughly cleaned using power washing or hand washing to remove dirt, mildew, and debris, ensuring proper paint adhesion.

2. Preparation

Proper prep is the foundation of a long-lasting finish.

Interior:

We repair minor imperfections, including cracks and holes, and smooth all surfaces. Stains are sealed to prevent bleed-through.

Exterior:

Loose or peeling paint is removed, surfaces are sanded, and gaps are sealed with high-quality caulk to prevent moisture intrusion.

3. Painting

This is where your vision comes to life.

Interior:

Repaired areas are primed, and premium-quality paint is applied evenly to achieve a smooth, consistent finish.

Exterior:

We apply high-performance coatings designed to withstand Florida’s climate, ensuring durability and a flawless appearance.

4. Clean-Up

We maintain a clean, organized workspace throughout the project.

Once the job is complete, we remove all materials, coverings, and debris—leaving your space spotless and ready to enjoy.

Final Inspection

Your satisfaction is our priority.

We conduct a detailed walkthrough with you to ensure every detail meets your expectations. Any final touch-ups are addressed promptly to guarantee a flawless result.
